## Objective
This section addresses **Part 2 of Module 2** and focuses on analyzing the
suitability of a **NoSQL database (MongoDB)** for managing a highly diverse and
evolving product catalog at FlexiMart.

The goal is to evaluate limitations of relational databases for this use case
and justify the adoption of MongoDB from a data architecture perspective.

---

## Scope of This Section
This part is **theory-focused** and includes:

- Analysis of why a traditional RDBMS struggles with diverse product attributes
- Explanation of how MongoDB addresses these challenges
- Identification of trade-offs when choosing MongoDB over MySQL/PostgreSQL
